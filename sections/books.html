Shadow of the Dragon

A Destiny Woven Into the Very Fabrics of Shadow.


Before Sarel takes her first breath, her fate is rewritten by forces older than time.

Born beneath a storm that fractures the earth and a ritual that reshapes destiny, she is bound to a power she does not yet understand.

In the quiet town of Emberdale, she knows nothing of the magic woven into her soul—only the warmth of Karl, the boy who has always been her safe harbor.

But when a terrible illness consumes Karl from the inside out, his very essence turning against him, Sarel’s world unravels.

Desperate to save him, she follows a trail of forbidden knowledge, uncovering whispers of forgotten gods, and the ancient magic that has shaped the world in silence.

Yet power is never without cost. To save Karl, she must enter a world where fate is a cage, where the gods punish those who defy them, and where magic does not grant mercy—it demands sacrifice.

As she unearths the truth of her birth, her gift, and the darkness awakening beneath the world, she faces a choice that will determine more than Karl’s fate.

Because some destinies cannot be escaped. And some fires refuse to be tamed.

Shadow of the Dragon is the first tale in The Weaver Chronicles, an epic dark fantasy saga exploring fate, forbidden power, and the fine line between salvation and ruin.
