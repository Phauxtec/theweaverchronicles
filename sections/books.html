The Weaver Chronicles: Shadow of the Dragon

A Destiny Woven in Shadows. A Fate That Refuses to Be Tamed.


Before Sarel takes her first breath, destiny wages war for her soul. Born beneath a storm that fractures the earth and a ritual that reshapes fate, she is bound to forces beyond her understanding.

For years, she knows nothing of the power that lingers beneath her skin, content with the quiet life of Emberdale and the love of Karl— the boy who has always been her safe harbor.

But when a strange illness consumes Karl, turning his very essence against him, Sarel’s world fractures. The answers she seeks lie beyond the veil of history— in whispers of forgotten gods, lost Weavers, and the unbreakable threads of fate that bind them all.

To save the man she loves, Sarel must step into a world where magic is both a gift and a curse, where bargains come at terrible costs, and where the gods do not forgive those who defy them. As she unravels the truth of her birth, her power, and the darkness stirring beneath the surface of the world, she will be forced to answer the one question that could unmake everything:

How far will she go to defy fate?

And can love truly stand against the will of the gods?

An Epic Dark Fantasy Saga


The Weaver Chronicles is a filled with ancient magic, looming catastrophe, and a heroine who must choose between love and power, fate and free will.

As the series unfolds, Sarel will face gods long thought dead, horrors that lurk beyond time, and the secrets hidden within the art of Weaving itself.

A tale of forbidden knowledge, sacrifice, and the fine line between salvation and destruction The threads of fate are unraveling.


Will she weave a new destiny or become its next victim?





The Weaver Chronicles: The Inquisition of Pallina

The Light Has Faded. Now, the Shadows Feast.


The halls were once a sanctuary— a place where prayers carried on whispered breath, where moonlight cast gentle blessings upon the faithful.

But something old stirs within its sacred stones, something hungry. The temple does not stand in ruin, yet its silence is not that of peace, but of waiting.

Summoned by a desperate plea for aid, Inquisitors of the Divine Order ride through the mountain pass under a swollen moon. Its cold light illuminates the path to a place that no longer welcomes the living.

The air is thick with the scent of incense and something far fouler, an undercurrent of rot that should not be. The temple is not abandoned. It is consumed.

Within its towering stone walls, the last priests remain. But are they keepers of the faith or prisoners of something far worse?

The braziers stand cold. The sacred wards flicker weakly against the encroaching dark. And somewhere beyond the veil of flickering candlelight, the hunger watches.

The Inquisition has come to cleanse what remains, to uncover the truth buried beneath centuries of devotion. But the deeper they step into the temple’s hollow corridors, the more they realize faith alone will not save them.

The blood on the stone is not yet dry. The whispers are not echoes of the past. And the thing that has awoken here does not fear the light.

The Descent Continues…


The Weaver Chronicles returns with a new tale of divine horror, ancient hunger, and the cost of faith in a world where gods do not answer.

As the past bleeds into the present, sanctuary becomes a tomb, and those who enter must ask themselves:

Do they bring salvation…

Or have they already been claimed?

The shadows are deeper than they seem. Enter at your own risk.
