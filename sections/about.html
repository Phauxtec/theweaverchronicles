About the Weaver Chronicles

Darkness Has Always Been Watching. Now, It Whispers Back.


There are forces older than time, lurking in the spaces between what is seen and what is known.

They do not rage like storms, nor do they announce themselves with fire and thunder.

They wait.

They watch.

And when the moment is right, they reach out...

Not with hands, but with whispers.

They shape the world in silence, twisting the fates of gods and mortals alike. A forgotten promise.

A shadow beneath the moonlight. A truth buried beneath the weight of centuries. Their touch is subtle…

In The Weaver Chronicles...

Power is never without cost, and survival is not the same as salvation.

To command the threads of fate is to unravel oneself.

To wield magic is to hear the voices of the forgotten.

To defy the gods is to become the thing they fear most.

This is not a story of heroes.

This is a story of descent

where ambition births ruin, where love is both a weapon and a curse, and where the greatest horrors do not rise from the darkness...

But from those who dare to reach beyond it.

The Weaver Chronicles:

A harrowing saga of psychological, physical, and cosmic horror.

It explores the depths of human depravity, the unbearable weight of existence, and the cost of defying the inevitable.

A world where ancient deities do not grant mercy, where forbidden knowledge reshapes the soul.

The greatest question is not who will survive…

Itnstead, What will be left of them if they do?
